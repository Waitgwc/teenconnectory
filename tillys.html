Minimum Age: 16+

Location: May Vary Depending Where you live

Job Type: Sales Associate

Primary Role: An entry-level position perfect for individuals with little-to-no training
sales associates remain responsible for impressing the values of the company
upon customers through excellent customer service and general store upkeep.
Applicants able to assume flexible schedules and plenty of weekend hours stand preferable hires.
Physical health also remains an important component to the job as long hours
of standing with little rest, bending, squatting, and lifting persist as regular
requirements of sales associates.
Additionally, the position demands patience and cheeriness when dealing with
a diverse multitude of customers, an ability to assist clientele in obtaining
merchandise for specific functions, demonstrating knowledge of fashion and sporting products,
maintaining the cleanliness and overall appearances of stores, and operating cash registers.
Usually a part-time position, sales associate jobs offer starting pay around minimum wage.
Experienced workers may receive raises based on performance and/or longevity with the company.

Requirements

  • Must be at least 16 years of age.
  • Exhibit good interpersonal and customer service skills.
  • Able to work in a fast paced environment and maintain energy and enthusiasm at all times.
  • Able to multi-task and focused on getting the job done the right way always
